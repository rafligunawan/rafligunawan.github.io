DYNAMICAL SYSTEMS BOOKS RECOMMENDATION

Books recommendations to learn dynamical systems:
1. Nonlinear Dynamics and Chaos - Strogatz
2. Discrete Chaos - Elyadi
3. Elements of Applied Bifurcation Theory - Kuznetsov
4. Introduction to the Modern Theory of Dynamical Systems - Katok & Hasselblatt
5. Elements of The Theory of Functions and Functional Analysis - Kolmogrov & Fomin
6. Lectures on Choquet's Theorem - Phelps
7. Topological Riesz Spaces and Measure Theory - Fremlin
8. An Introduction to Ergodic Theory - Walters
9. Ergodic Theory: With a View Towards Number Theory - Einsiedler & Ward
10. Data-Driven Science and Engineering: Machine Learning, Dynamical Systems, and Control - Kutz & et al
