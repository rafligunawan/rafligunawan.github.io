THEORITICAL PHYSICS BOOKS RECOMMENDATION

Books recommendations to learn theoritical physics:
1. Classical Mechanics - Goldstein
2. Classical Electrodynamics - Jackson
3. Lectures on Quantum Mechanics - Weinberg
4. Elementary Statistical Physics - Kittel
5. The Quantum Theory of Fields - Weinberg
6. Quantum Field Theory and the Standard - Schwartz
7. Special Relativity in General Frames: From Particles to Astrophysics - Gourgoulhon
8. The Geometry of Minkowski Spacetime - Naber
9. General Relativity for Mathematicians - Sachs & Wu
10. String Theory and M-Theory - Becker
